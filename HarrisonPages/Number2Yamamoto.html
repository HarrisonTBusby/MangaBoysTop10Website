Number 2: Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto

Bankai: Zanka No Tachi

"Zanka No Tachi: East"

"Zanka No Tachi: West"

"Zanka No Tachi: South"

"Zanka No Tachi: North"


Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto is the former Captain Commander of the Gotei 13. His bankai is Zanka No Tachi and it is split into 4 parts: North, South, East, and West. The first part is Zanka No Tachi: East. Zanka No Tachi: East concentrates intense flames along the tip of the blade and anything the tip of the blade touches is eradicated to nothingness. The next is Zanka No Tachi: West. Zanka No Tachi: West starts with intense heat reaching temperatures of 15,000,000 degrees engulfing his whole body making it impossible to get near him without turning into dust. The next is Zanka No Tachi: South. Zanka No Tachi: South starts with Yamamoto summoning every corpse that has ever been killed by his sword (10 trillion) and he can use the corpses to attack his enemies. The last is Zanka No Tachi: North; an slash that concentrated all the fire and heat that he has that incenerates whatever it touches out of existence.
