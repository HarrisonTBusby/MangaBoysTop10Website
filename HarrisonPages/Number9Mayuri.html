Number 9: Mayuri Kurotsuchi

"Captain of Squad 12: Mayuri Kurotsuchi"

"Modified Bankai: Modified Bankai: Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō: Matai Fukuin Shōtai"

"Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō"


Mayuri Kurotsuchi is the Captain of Squad 12 in the Gotei 13. His modified bankai is Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō: Matai Fukuin Shōtai. His bankai takes the form of an enormous, heavyset, purple-skinned baby dressed in a golden loincloth and with golden fabric covering its pectorals. It has a misshapen head with completely white eyes, which is adorned with two branches of a balancing scale holding up large boxes, and its belly has a large vertical gash in the center with the kanji for "Conceal" written with the individual kanji on either side. The baby rests on a large pile of thick umbilical cords with visible veins. The special ability of his bankai is that it can modify itself to counter any opponent it goes against.
