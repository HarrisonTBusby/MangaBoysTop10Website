Number 3: Ichigo Kurosaki

Bankai: True Tensa Zangetsu

"Horn of Salvation"

"Gran Rey Getsuga"


Ichigo Kurosaki is the main character of Bleach (G.O.A.T.). Ichigo's true bankai is a unique bankai in that it is only shown very briefly but we know through context clues that its super powerful. Ichigo's true bankai is called Tensa Zangetsu. Tensa Zangetsu increases not only the physical abilities of Ichigo but also his physical appearance as well. In this true Bankai, Ichigo has the Horn of Salvation, a representation of his Hollow powers that are inside of him. His other main ability is Gran Ray Getsuga, a Getsuga Tenso (his main ability) with a Gran Rey Cero (basically a laser beam that destroys countries).
