Manga #10 Blue Period

Synopsis:

Yatora Yaguchi is a delinquent with excellent grades, however he is unmotivated to find his true calling in life. While mulling over his predicament, Yatora finds himself staring at a vibrant landscape of Shibuya early one morning. Unable to express how he feels about the sight, he picks up a paintbrush hoping his thoughts will be conveyed on canvas. After recieving praise for his work, the joy he feels sends him on a journey of discovering what might be his true calling.
