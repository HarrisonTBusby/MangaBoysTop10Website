Manga #6 Bleach

Synopsis:

For as long as he can remember, Ichigo Kurosaki has been able to see the spirits of the dead. One day, he returns home to find an intruder in his room, a Soul Reaper named Rukia Kuchiki, who is tasked with helping souls pass over to the other side. After an encounter where they are attacked by a Hollow - an evil spirit known for consuming souls - Ichigo takes up her blade inheritng half of her power, becoming a Substitute Soul Reaper himself. He must now take up her duties of exterminating Hollows and protecting spirits, both living and dead.
