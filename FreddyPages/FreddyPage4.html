Manga # 8 Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

Synopsis:

This is the story of the Joestar Bloodline, beginning with Jonathan Joestar. He discovers his adoptive brother Dio’s plot to take everything the Joestar’s have as his own. This greed brings him to take on an ancient Aztec curse. Jonathan’s fight against Dio has rippling effect, thus bringing the rest of the Joestar bloodline after him to take on newfound powers to fight against this ancient evil.
