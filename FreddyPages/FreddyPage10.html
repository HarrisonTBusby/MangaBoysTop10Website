Manga #2 Vinland Saga

Synopsis:

Thorfinn, son of one of the Vikings greatest warriors, is among the finest fighters in the band of mercenaries led by a man named Askeladd. However, he is not a part of the group for the plunder it entails. Instead, for having caused his family great tragedy, the boy has vowed to kill Askeladd in a fair duel. Not yet skilled enough to defeat him, but unable to abandon his vengeance, Thorfinn spends his childhood with the mercenary crew, honing his skills on the battlefield. A bloody epic in an era where violence, madness, and injustice where inescapable.
