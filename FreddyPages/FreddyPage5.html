Manga # 7 Nana

Synopsis:

Nana Komatsu is a naive, unmotivated girl who spends her days chasing one crush after another. Nana Osaki, on the other hand, is feisty and prideful. She is at an ultimatum where she must choose between her boyfriend and her musical career, where she chooses the latter. Two girls with the same name, but very different aspirations find themselves sitting together on their journey to the city. A deep and unique bond is then forged, where they will support each other in this saga of love, music, friendship and heartbreak.
