Manga #5 Hell's Paradise

Synopsis:

Gabimaru, a ninja from a village called Iwagakure, finds himself on death row after being set up by his fellow ninja.Tired of killing and betrayal, he wants to die. However no method of execution works on him. Realizing that his love for his wife is keeping him alive. The executioner proposes he join an expedition to an island south of Japan in search of the Elixir of Life in exchange for a full pardon from the Shogunate. However, this island isn’t a normal island: it’s believed to be Paradise. But is it?
